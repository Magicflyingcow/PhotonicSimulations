Interactive X-ray CT physics simulator

Forward model: Beer–Lambert attenuation \(I = I_0 e^{-\int \mu ds}\), Poisson counting noise, logarithmic line-integral recovery, then filtered backprojection (ramp filter) to reconstruct attenuation \(\mu\).

Attenuation map units are linear attenuation coefficient \(\mu\) in mm⁻¹. Values are representative: soft tissue ~0.020 mm⁻¹, bone ~0.038 mm⁻¹ at diagnostic X-ray energies.
